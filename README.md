# Neon Debris

A cyberpunk auto-battler roguelike. Recruit operatives aboard a decaying orbital station, apply upgrades, position your squad, and survive three sectors of escalating hostile encounters.

Built by **42 Pixel Studios**.

## Features

**10 Operative Classes** — Psion, Medtech, Street Samurai, Riot Frame, Railgun Sniper, Chemist, Ghost, Drone Rigger, Enforcer, and Combat Drone. Each spawns with a random callsign and one of 3 ability variants.

**Synergy System** — Frontline, Sharpshooter, Phantom, Nanotech, Cyber, and Toxic synergies activate when you field matching operatives.

**Roguelike Map** — Navigate 3 acts (Lower Decks, Mid Ring, Command Deck) with battle nodes, rest stops, black markets, treasure caches, and unknown signals. Each act ends with a boss encounter.

**Meta-Progression** — Earn Datakeys every run. Spend them in the Cortex on firmware upgrades, operative dossiers, augment blueprints, threat protocols, commander mastery, and more.

**Commander System** — 6 backgrounds (Street Fixer, Corp Defector, Trauma Surgeon, Black Hat, Corpo Merc, Netdasher) with unique perks and 10-level mastery tracks.

**Combat** — Continuous movement with tick-based combat, 9 shader effects, directional blood splatter, death animations, cyberpunk projectile effects, and speed controls (0.5x/1x/2x/4x).
